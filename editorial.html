Know The Board

The CityU LR is edited by law students under the guidance of experienced faculty members. The student members of the Board are supported by a Faculty Editor, the IBA and an international pool of Peer Reviewers.

 

The Editorial Board manages all aspects related to the publication of the CityU LR, i.e., selecting papers for publication, editing selected papers, liaising with authors, supervising production with press, and marketing the journal.
